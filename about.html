About TicketWave

TicketWave is your go-to platform for buying and selling tickets to exciting events in the East Rand. We connect event organizers with eager attendees, making it easy to discover and attend the best events in your area.

Our mission is to provide a seamless and reliable ticket marketplace where you can find tickets to a wide variety of events, from live music and sports to theater and community gatherings.

Whether you're looking for a fun night out, a family adventure, or a chance to learn something new, TicketWave has something for everyone.

Our Story

TicketWave was founded in 2023 by a group of passionate event enthusiasts who wanted to make it easier for people to discover and attend amazing events in the East Rand. We saw a need for a platform that would bring together event organizers and attendees in a convenient and user-friendly way.

Since our launch, we've been growing rapidly and have helped thousands of people connect with their favorite events. We're committed to providing a safe and secure platform for buying and selling tickets, and we're always working to improve our services and features.

Our Values

  • Customer Focus: We put our customers first and strive to provide them with the best possible experience.
  • Reliability: We're committed to providing a reliable and trustworthy platform for buying and selling tickets.
  • Innovation: We're constantly innovating and improving our services to meet the evolving needs of our customers.
  • Community: We believe in building a strong community of event enthusiasts and supporting local events.

Our Team

Our team is made up of experienced professionals who are passionate about events and technology. We're dedicated to providing you with the best possible service and support.
